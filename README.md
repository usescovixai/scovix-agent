# SCOVIX AI

> Turn Your Camera Roll Into On-Chain Income.

Scovix AI is a decentralized data marketplace built on Solana that rewards creators for contributing original photos and videos to power the next generation of AI models.

Instead of letting your media sit unused in your camera roll, Scovix transforms your personal content into a productive digital asset. Upload once, contribute to AI datasets, and automatically earn SOL whenever your content is licensed through the protocol.

---

# 🚀 What is Scovix?

Artificial Intelligence depends on massive amounts of high-quality training data.

Today, most contributors receive little to no compensation for the images and videos that help build AI systems.

Scovix changes that.

By connecting creators directly with AI data demand, Scovix creates a transparent marketplace where contributors retain ownership while earning rewards for valuable media.

```text
Capture
    ↓
Upload
    ↓
AI Validation
    ↓
Dataset Curation
    ↓
AI Licensing
    ↓
Automatic SOL Rewards
```

The protocol enables a fair data economy where every contributor participates in the value generated by AI datasets.
